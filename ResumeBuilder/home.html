Key points to include in resume

Personal Information Your name, phone number, email address, and optionally, your LinkedIn profile or personal website.
Resume Summary Or Objective A brief statement summarizing your professional background, skills, and career goals.
Skills Section List relevant technical, soft, and transferable skills that are applicable to the job you're applying for.
Work Experience Detail your work history in reverse chronological order, including job titles, company names, locations, and dates of employment.
Education Include your educational background, starting with your highest level of education attained.
Certifications and Training If you have relevant certifications or have completed any additional training programs, include them in a separate section.
Projects or Portfolio If applicable, showcase any relevant projects you've worked on or a portfolio of your work.
Awards and Honors Mention any awards, honors, or recognitions you've received throughout your career or academic journey.
Volunteer Experience Include any volunteer work or community service experience that demonstrates relevant skills or qualities.
Professional Memberships If you're a member of any professional organizations or associations related to your field, mention them.
Additional Sections Depending on your background and the job you're applying for, you may include additional sections such as languages spoken, interests/hobbies, or publications.
